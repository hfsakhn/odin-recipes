Pizza

Description!

This is Hafsa Khan's famous pizza recipe. Hafsa Khan loves pizza more than anything, so she is just the expert you need for all your pizza questions.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Olives (duh)
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions

Steps

  1. First, preheat your oven to 460 degrees
  2. Roll out your dough to a nice circle size.
  3. Generously add pizza sauce all over your dough
  4. Sprinkle on as much cheese as you would like (i like a lot bc im fat lol)
  5. Get to cutting your veggies
  6. Add all your veggies on top!
  7. Put your pizza in the oven for 20 minutes
  8. Take your pizza out the oven and enjoy!
